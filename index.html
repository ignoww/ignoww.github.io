Introduce

I am a third-year MS/Ph.D student in electrical and computer engineering (ECE) at Seoul National University (SNU), advised by Prof. Se Young Chun. I earned my B.S degree in electrical and computer engineering (ECE) from Seoul National University (SNU).

Research Interests

My research interests broadly lie in "efficient" AI. I focus on making AI resource-efficient, computation-efficient, and model size-efficient in all aspects. I am interested in both Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Computer Vision (CV), but my current focus is on CV tasks. Specifically, my work explores the following areas:

  • Efficient AI
  • Model Compression
  • Quantization & Pruning

but not limited to.

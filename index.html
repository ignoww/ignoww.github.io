Introduce

I am a fourth-year MS/Ph.D student in electrical and computer engineering (ECE) at Seoul National University (SNU), advised by Prof. Se Young Chun. I earned my B.S degree in electrical and computer engineering (ECE) from Seoul National University (SNU).

Research Interests

My research interests broadly lie in "efficient" AI. I aim to make AI systems resource-, computation-, and parameter-efficient across all stages of development and deployment. While I am interested in both Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Computer Vision (CV), my current focus is on CV tasks. Specifically, my research explores, but is not limited to, the following areas:

  • Efficient Deep Learning
  • Model Compression Techniques
  • Quantization and Pruning
